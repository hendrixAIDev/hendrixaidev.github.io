What ChurnPilot Does

📚 Card Library — Start in Seconds

Choose from 50+ card templates from Chase, Amex, Capital One, Citi, Discover, Bank of America, Bilt, US Bank, Wells Fargo, and more. Every benefit, credit, and perk is already filled in. Just pick your cards and go.

🤖 Ask AI — Just Describe the Card

Found a new card offer? Paste the URL, describe the card, or just name it — ChurnPilot's AI extracts the annual fee, benefits, signup bonus, and spend requirements automatically. It checks the card library first for a verified match, so you get accurate data without typing a thing. Separate quotas keep things fair for quick lookups vs. bulk spreadsheet imports.

📊 Benefit Tracking — See What You're Missing

Track monthly credits, annual credits, and special perks across all your cards. ChurnPilot shows you what you've used and — more importantly — what you haven't. That's the money you're leaving behind.

🎯 Signup Bonus Tracker — Never Miss a Deadline

Track your minimum spend requirements, deadlines, and progress toward every signup bonus. ChurnPilot tells you exactly how much more you need to spend and how many days you have left.

🏦 Chase 5/24 Tracker — Know When You're Eligible

Chase denies applications if you've opened 5+ cards in 24 months. ChurnPilot tracks your application history and tells you exactly when you can apply again.

💰 Portfolio Analytics — Your True ROI

See your total annual fees, total benefits value, net value, and ROI across your entire portfolio — at a glance. Finally know whether your cards are working for you or against you.

🔄 Product Changes — Upgrade or Downgrade in Place

Changed your card to a different tier? ChurnPilot handles product changes correctly — upgrading or downgrading the card template and benefits in place, preserving your history without making you start over.

✏️ Editable Annual Fees — Your Actual Cost, Not a Generic Number

Retention offers, fee waivers, and first-year discounts mean your annual fee might not match the published rate. Now you can edit the annual fee on any card so ChurnPilot's ROI calculation reflects what you're actually paying.

🗑️ Bulk Card Management — Clean Up Fast

Closed several cards at once? Select multiple cards and delete them in a single action. No more removing cards one by one.

📂 Spreadsheet Import — Bring Your Data

Already tracking your cards in a spreadsheet? Upload your CSV, XLSX, or TSV file and import everything in one step. ChurnPilot replaces your spreadsheet — it doesn't make you start from scratch.

🌐 Bilingual — English & 中文

Full Chinese language support with a one-click toggle. Every label, every feature, every detail — in your language.
